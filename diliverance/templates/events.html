How It Started

The Call To Missions

An open call to embark on a mission trip to Timor Leste stirred a desire in our hearts to go out to the nations - to reach out to the lost and the broken, and share the love of Christ.

At first, each of us was hesitant.

"What if I end up going alone? What if I don't have the time or resources?""

But what we didn't know was that God was already at work in the hearts of our friends, family and church community. We were encouraged by each other's desire to go into the mission field, as well as God's providence through the cheerful giving of friends and family, and it soon became clear that He was calling us to go together as a missional cell group.
