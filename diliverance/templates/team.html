Testimonies

How has God spoken to you through Project Diliverance 2023?

Goer, Worship Team

Keiko Ang

Despite having been to Indonesia countless times to visit my extended family, I was still taken aback by the harsh living conditions of Timor Leste. Electricity blackouts were common, hot water was a luxury, and the abundance of cockcroaches kept me constantly on my toes due to my terrible fear of insects.

However, what really stood out to me and challenged my faith was the resilience of the Timorese in serving God and His people, and to worship Him joyfully amidst these circumstances. If I had been put in the same circumstances as them - facing persistent, violent persecution for my faith - would I still have been able to worship God with that same passion? How can I learn to be more grateful for what God has blessed me with back in Singapore - to be more willing to serve Him joyfully with these gifts?

Goer, Worship Team

Khanh Bui

Growing up in a rural mountainous area of Vietnam, I understand the plight of those who grow up in poverty, but I was blessed to be born into a Christian family, and to be able to know about God. I know that many in Timor-Leste are not as fortunate.

During my trip there, I saw firsthand the struggles they face—not just physically, but spiritually. The lack of exposure to the gospel is one of their greatest challenges. God’s love has been such a source of strength in my life, and I feel a deep conviction to share that same love with the people of Timor-Leste, especially the children there. God has called me to be a "goer," not just to observe but to truly learn, connect, and share His word.

This trip has really strengthened my desire to bring hope and the gospel to those who haven’t yet heard it. I want to show them the love of God that I’ve been so blessed with, and to walk alongside them in their journey toward Him.
