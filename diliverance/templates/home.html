What is Project Diliverance?

Loving Our Neighbours

Project Diliverance is a youth-led mission trip to Dili, Timor Leste, in support of Cocoon's mission to change lives through education. Our trip will include fun-filled activities such as games, dances, and skits, as well as the sharing of our personal testimonies, and small group prayer sessions, which will culminate in a Christmas event for the children and youth. We seek to connect with and show love to the local children and youth, for we were first loved by the Father and have been commanded to "love your neighbour as yourself" (Matthew 22:39).
